Services

We offer automatic and manual screen-printing capabilities to give you consistent quality and fast turn-around on small and larger orders. We have the latest, state-of-the-art technology for printing high-quality, multi-color designs, ensuring that every order is done quickly and to your standards.

Screen Printing

Screen printing is our number one specialty, and your solution for when you need any customized gear delivered fast. No matter how quickly we print and deliver an order, we ensure our production team has triple checked each item for excellent accuracy and quality.

DIRECT TO GARMENT

Direct to Garment, or DTG printing, is a process by which a digital image is printed directly onto your apparel using the newest technology in apparel printing

EMBROIDERY

Embroidering your design adds an extra level of sophistication, making it the perfect choice for businesses, school groups.

Design Review and Repair

To ensure the best possible outcome for each order, our professional in-house designers review placement, sizing, and color on every design our customers submit.

PERMAPRESS​

PermaPress is a print method where full color images are digitally printed onto high quality, light-weight vinyl. The image is then cut out precisely and pressed onto your apparel. the image will last for years to come.

FOIL PRINTING​

If you want something eye-catching, foil printing is the way to go. Foil printing incorporates a metallic finish that adds a shiny flair to your designs.
